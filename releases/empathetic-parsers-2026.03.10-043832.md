# WitFoo Parsers v2026.03.10-043832

**Release Date:** 2026-03-10

## Summary
This release introduces a new auto-generated parser, `witfooad`, designed to capture and classify previously unknown log messages detected in customer environments. The parser ingests via UDP and routes unrecognized log data into the GenericLog stream, ensuring that no log traffic is silently dropped and enabling analysts to triage and promote these signals as new formats are identified.

## What's New
### Features
- **New parser: `witfooad`** — Auto-generated empathetic parser for handling unknown log messages observed in production customer environments.
  - **Stream:** GenericLog
  - **Message Type:** Unknown
  - **Entrypoint:** `udp_source`
  - Ensures previously unclassified UDP log traffic is captured, normalized, and made available for review rather than being discarded at ingestion.
