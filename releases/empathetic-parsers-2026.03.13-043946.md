# WitFoo Parsers v2026.03.13-043946

**Release Date:** 2026-03-13

## Summary
This release introduces a new auto-generated parser, `witfooad`, designed to capture and classify previously unknown log messages detected in customer environments. The parser ingests via UDP and routes unrecognized log data into the GenericLog stream, ensuring that no log traffic is silently dropped and enabling analysts to triage and promote these signals as new formats are identified.
